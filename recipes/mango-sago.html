Mango Sago

What is Mango Sago?

Mango sago is a refreshing dessert made with fresh mangoes and small tapioca pearls. Itâ€™s the perfect light and fruity pudding or drink.

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup small or medium Tapioca
  • 5 cups water
  • 250ml all purpose cream
  • 190g sweetened condensed milk
  • 2-3 Mangoes
  • 1 jar Nata de Coco
  • 1 jar Macapuno
Steps:
  1. In a pot, bring water to a boil. Add tapioca pearls and cook, stirring occasionally on medium heat for half an hour. Remove pot from heat and let sit for 5 minutes. Transfer tapioca pearls to a strainer, rinse under cold running water. Strain the tapioca and add to a large bowl.
  2. Add all purpose cream, sweeetned condensed milk, nata de Coco and macapuno into the bowl and stir.
  3. Add diced mangoes to the tapioca mixture and stir.
  4. Refrigerate for a few hours before serving.
